Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing

Our prenatal DNA testing uses a simple blood sample from the mother to determine paternity safely during pregnancy.

Key Benefits:

  • No risk to the baby
  • Highly accurate results
  • Confidential and discreet process

When Can Testing Be Done?

As early as 7–9 weeks into pregnancy (consultation required).
